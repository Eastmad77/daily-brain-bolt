What is BrainBolt?

The Daily BrainBolt is a bite-sized quiz that mixes world trivia, logic, language, science, and more — designed to be fun, fast, and replayable. No accounts. No friction. Just a quick spark ⚡ every day.

How it works

  • Tap Start Quiz to begin today’s set.
  • A 10-second timer keeps each question snappy.
  • Instant feedback with short explanations.
  • Share with friends and compare scores.

Where do questions come from?

Questions refresh daily from a curated bank maintained by the BrainBolt team. We aim for a balance of difficulty and variety so there’s always something fresh to learn.
