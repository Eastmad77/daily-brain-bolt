What is BrainBolt?

The Daily BrainBolt is a lightweight, fast quiz you can take in under a minute. Questions are pulled from a curated bank and surfaced each day — so you can build knowledge and keep a streak going.

How it works

  • A fresh set of questions is published daily.
  • You get a 10-second timer per question (with smooth progress).
  • Missed a question? Review the answer and try the next.

Built as a modern PWA, BrainBolt works great on mobile and can be installed to your home screen.
