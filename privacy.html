Privacy Policy

How we handle your data.

Data we collect: Basic usage analytics (e.g., sessions, device type) to improve gameplay. If you sign in, we store your profile (name, email, UID) and game stats.

How we use it: To run the app, save your scores, send daily reminders if enabled, and improve BrainBolt.

Sharing: We do not sell personal data. We use trusted processors (e.g., Firebase) to provide the service.

Cookies / Storage: Used for preferences (theme/audio), auth sessions, and PWA caching.

Security: We apply industry-standard measures; no system is 100% secure.

Your rights: Request access or deletion by emailing support@dailybrainbolt.com.

Changes: We may update this policy and post changes here.
