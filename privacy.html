Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-12

Overview

Brain ⚡ Bolt (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using our website or app (the “Service”), you agree to this policy.

What We Collect

  • Usage data (minimal): basic analytics such as page views or feature usage (aggregated/anonymous).
  • Quiz session data: when you finish a game, we may store the date, score, elapsed time, and non-identifying session info in our database to power leaderboards and trends.
  • Notifications (optional): if you enable web push, your browser may create a device token so we can send a “daily quiz is ready” message.
  • Sign-in (optional): if you choose to sign in (e.g., with Google), we receive your basic profile (name, email, avatar) for account features like syncing progress.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the quiz experience.
  • Deliver optional daily notifications (if you opted in).
  • Maintain security, debug issues, and prevent abuse.
  • Offer account features like saved progress (if signed in).

Legal Bases

If you are in the EEA/UK, our legal bases include: legitimate interests (to operate and improve the Service), consent (for notifications and sign-in), and contract (to provide features you request).

Sharing & Service Providers

We do not sell your data. We may use trusted providers to host infrastructure or deliver services (e.g., Firebase for authentication, database, and notifications). These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business needs (troubleshooting, legal compliance). You may request deletion of your account data if you signed in.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data. No system is 100% secure. Please use strong, unique passwords for any connected accounts.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Notifications: you can enable/disable web push in your browser settings and within the app.
  • Access/Deletion: if you signed in, you can request access or deletion of your account data.
  • Consent withdrawal: you may withdraw consent (e.g., notifications, sign-in) at any time.
  • Regional rights: depending on your location (e.g., EEA/UK/California), you may have additional rights such as data portability or objection. Contact us to exercise your rights.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We implement appropriate safeguards for such transfers where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted within the Service and the “Last updated” date will change.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at support@dailybrainbolt.com.
