Privacy Policy

The Daily BrainBolt (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our quiz application.

The Daily BrainBolt does not require user accounts or collect personally identifiable information.

The app may store small amounts of data locally (such as progress, scores, or preferences) using browser storage. This data stays on your device and is not shared with us.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services (such as hosting via Netlify or analytics) which have their own privacy policies. These providers may collect technical information such as IP address or browser type.

Children’s Privacy

The Daily BrainBolt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
