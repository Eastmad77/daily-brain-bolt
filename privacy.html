Privacy

How we handle your data.

We respect your privacy. This policy describes the information we collect and how we use it.

Third-Party Services. We may use analytics and notification services to deliver features and measure usage. Their privacy policies apply.

Contact. For privacy questions or requests, email support@dailybrainbolt.com.
